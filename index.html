Sam Macasieb Timeline

Early on August 8, 2014, Sam Macasieb was found unconscious by a coworker at the West Oakland USPS processing and distribution center.

  • 8/08/14 3:00am

    Sam Macasieb is found unconscious on the ground

    Some time between 3:00am and 3:30am, a worker at the West Oakland facility finds Macasieb lying motionless on the ground. The coworker yells to another coworker, who contacts his supervisor. At the same time, another employee hears the call and runs to her supervisor. That supervisor assesses Macasieb and waits for a higher manager to arrive. Then, the postal police are contacted.

  • 8/08/14 3:53am

    911 receives call

    Following facility policy, the on-site Postal Police dial 911. Dispatch records confirm the call was at 3:53am. Because the Postal Police are dialing from a landline on a different floor from Macasieb, they have trouble relaying important information to the dispatchers, such as where Macasieb's injuries appear to be or whether his eyes were open or not.

  • 8/08/14 4:00am

    Paramedics Arrive

    An ambulance transports Macasieb to Highland Hospital in Oakland. Macasieb is in a coma and won't wake up. Up to an hour has passed since he was first discovered.

  • 8/17/14 10 days later

    Macasieb dies

    After ten days in a coma, Macasieb's family removes him from life support. The official cause of death is a blunt injury to the head, likely from a fall.
